About

BelloSounds Records was born as a place, a creative space, more than a label. In the ’90s, when everything began, the club was a temple.
Between 1993 and 2003, nights were written on vinyl, and every mix was a drop capable of changing the atmosphere. Then, that world went silent, not because the passion faded, but because the context changed:
clubs lost their soul, radios lost their curiosity, and music stopped being a collective experience. Today, BelloSounds closes that circle again.
It gathers what was left unfinished, giving voice to a generation once interrupted, but now reborn, evolved.

BelloSounds is a fragment of that time, filtered through the awareness of today. A bridge between memory and the future. A sound that doesn’t imitate, but it remembers where it came from.

More than a label, a creative lab. Two sides, one vision.
